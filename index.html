Longest Continuous Distance on Grid Visualization

The grid is randomly generated each time the page is refreshed and passed to the longest continuous distance function.
The visualization speed can be adjusted to closely observe the traversal process and verify correctness.
The traversal order follows this sequence: right -> down -> diagonal(down & right) -> diagonal(down & left) ->
(if not avoiding redundant paths then) left -> up -> diagonal(up & left) -> diagonal (up & right)
